### Your Voice for Justice

VoiceFIR is a voice-first system that helps citizens generate a structured First Information Report (FIR) by simply describing an incident in their own words.

FIR filing in India is often intimidating due to complex legal language, lack of awareness, and emotional stress after an incident. VoiceFIR removes these barriers by allowing users to speak freely while the system handles legal structuring and formatting.

---

## üö® Problem Statement
Many crimes go unreported or are delayed because FIR filing:
- Requires legal knowledge
- Involves lengthy forms and procedures
- Feels intimidating for first-time complainants
- Is not accessible for all users

---

## üí° Proposed Solution
VoiceFIR enables users to:
- Speak or type incident details in natural language
- Automatically generate a legally structured FIR draft
- Review and validate the draft
- Download the FIR in a proper PDF format

The platform simplifies FIR filing while maintaining clarity and structure.
